Taste of Pematangsiantar

A culinary journey through my hometown's best dishes.

Roti Ketawa

Roti ketawa is a snack typical of North Sumatra in the form of small round fried doughnuts with a cracked surface, making them look like they are laughing. They have a savory sweet taste with sesame seeds sprinkled on the surface.

MIESO

Miso Siantar is one of the legendary foods from Pematangsiantar City, North Sumatra. At first glance, this dish resembles simple noodle soup, but it has a distinctive clear, savory, and fragrant broth. The broth is usually made from free-range chicken or chicken bones that are cooked for a long time to produce a rich flavor.

Mie Gomak

Mie Gomak Siantar is a typical Batak Toba dish that is popular in Pematangsiantar. It is often referred to as “Batak spaghetti” because the noodles are thick and long, similar to spaghetti. This dish is usually served with a spicy coconut milk sauce seasoned with andaliman, which gives it a unique savory and spicy flavor.

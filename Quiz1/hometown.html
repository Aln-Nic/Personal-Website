My Hometown

Pematangsiantar, North Sumatera, Indonesia

Pematangsiantar is the second largest city in North Sumatra after Medan, known for its cool climate and cultural diversity. The city is also the gateway to Lake Toba and has a unique mode of transportation in the form of classic BSA motorized rickshaws.
